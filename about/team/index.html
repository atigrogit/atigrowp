Team:
who delivers.

We do things the way they should be done. We strive for mastery across all of our expertise areas. We want our clients to be confident in our work and the quality we deliver. At Atigro, we do things a better way.

We are a team of experts who demonstrate honesty, helpfulness, and a strong work ethic every day. We hire good people we enjoy working with, and we think you’ll like them too. We are constantly learning and improving. It is in our DNA. We trust each other and that allows us to produce the best results for our clients. We are so proud of the success our clients achieve.

KEN FISCHER

CEO

Ken Fischer is founder and CEO of Atigro, a Washington DC-based agency specializing in web development, software development, mobile apps, and digital marketing for companies and nonprofits nationwide.

He leads our team of engineers and technical managers in the US and Hyderabad, India. Ken’s business acumen combined with his technical expertise ensures that technology is thoughtfully applied to create long-term value for clients. He has implemented over 100 enterprise solutions successfully.

Ken attended Washington University School of Medicine in St. Louis and the University of Maryland Baltimore County.

CHIEF DIGITAL OFFICER

Clark creates an SEO roadmap to increase search visibility, traffic, conversions, and revenue. He has worked in most industries with extensive work in automotive, ecommerce, financial, healthcare, hospitality, and media.

Clark is a top SEO expert in the U.S. and protégé of Eric Enge, The Art of SEO lead author. Ask about his proven track record of organic search growth that has transformed enterprises and his work as a trusted advisor to executives, CMOs, digital, and internal SEO teams.
