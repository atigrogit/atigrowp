01.19.21 Impact of the 12/27 COVID Relief Bill on the SBA’s Paycheck Protection Program

Download Presentation
Watch Video

Close

Join us for our virtual coffee to hear Rod Johnson of the US Small Business Administration share how the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2021, H.R. 133, which was signed into law on December 27, 2020, impacts the SBA’s small business programs, including an additional $284 billion for a new round of Paycheck Protection Program (PPP) loans and updated guidelines for PPP Loan Forgiveness. Rod will probe into the pressing questions business leaders need answers to.

Have a specific question you’d like answered? Share your questions as you register so our speaker can tailor the discussion to what matters most or ask during our live Q&A.
